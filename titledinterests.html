Social Issues


As a teacher , I made sure my teaching was accessible to my students of varying socio-economic backgrounds. I left teaching because I realized that the students in schools like mine needed more than "the 3 Rs," Reading, wRiting, and aRithmatic"

Pursuing my social work degree made me more aware about the issues around me. I had the opportunities to work with vulnerable populations like individuals experiencing homelessness and our older adult population.

Having worked in these areas, I learned to be more politically engaged and aware in order to be pro-active and better helper.
